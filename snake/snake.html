Instructions

  1. Input your username to play the game.
  2. Click the 'play' button.
  3. Use your arrows keys or 'WASD' keys to move around.
  4. Eat apples to grow and increase score.
  5. Avoid colliding with your own tail.
  6. To rewind, click the 'Rewind' button and adjust the slider to turn back in time within 5 seconds.
